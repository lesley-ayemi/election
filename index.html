About Him

Who Is He?

Former Senior Special Assistant to the Governor of Delta State on Peace Building and Conflict Resolution, Chief Samson Gordons Okomitie has emerged Patron of the South-South Solidarity Youth Movement, a body of progressive youths from all the states of the south-south region committed towards strengthening regional stability and development in Nigeria, with the new patron pledging to work with the youths to promote the interests of youths in politics and other realms of human endeavour. Chief Okomitie, who holds the prestigious chieftaincy titles of Ede-Dorin of Ughelli Kingdom and Ede-Oghene of Ughievwen Kingdom, promised to leverage on his position in the Dr. Ifeanyi Okowa’s administration to improve the lots of Delta youths particularly youths from Ughelli South LGA, urging the youths to step up advocacy against vices and criminal tendencies among youths in the state.
